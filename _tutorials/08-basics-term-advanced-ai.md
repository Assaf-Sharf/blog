`Determining the level of AI.`

The deciding factor in determining the level of AI is [AM{Observer}](/tutorials/basics-term-observer).

[AM{Observer}](/tutorials/basics-term-observer) determinate the level of AI based on [AM{World}](/tutorials/basics-am-world-and-form).

In practice, this involves establishing rules in the form of tests.

Test result become the level of AI.
