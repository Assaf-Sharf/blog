#### Requisition 096882

As the Director of Business Development, you will have an extraordinary opportunity to deliver on our mission to advance the role of data science in biology, medicine, and health care, and ultimately transform health care delivery for millions of patients throughout the world. 
You will build, manage, and leverage relationships on behalf of the Center for Data Intensive Science on trust, respect, and a mutual appreciation of value. You will identify short-term opportunities and lay the groundwork for the creation of long-term strategies. You will proactively identify industry trends and opportunities by researching industry and related events, publications, and announcements. You will build our market position by locating, developing, defining, negotiating, and closing business relationships. You will take ownership of growth strategies and financial targets. 
<br>
<br>
Duties will include: 

- Executes the strategic business development plan while working with key internal stakeholders 

- Provides project management for strategic projects 

- Screens potential business relationships by analyzing market strategies, requirements, potential, and financials; evaluating options; resolving internal priorities 

- Understands competitors' strengths and weaknesses relative to platform alternatives to create and revise the strategy as needed. 

- Develops negotiating strategies by analyzing the integration of new venture with organizational strategies; evaluates risks and potentials; articulates value proposition for partners; integrates contract requirements with business operations 

- Handles complex business requirements and coordinates appropriate ecosystem players globally 

- Formulates and drives compelling communications to potential partners 

- Continuously interfaces with business partners to recognize and drive timely feedback on issues 

- Documents and maintains record of customer relationship management, pipeline management and prospect action plans 

- Collects and applies metrics to measure effectiveness of operations and support improved performance 

- Prepares and presents strategic objectives, forecasts, and comprehensive reports 

- Enhances organizational reputation by representing the organization in an enthusiastic, professional, and respectful manner 

- Protects organization by maintaining a high level of confidentiality  
<br>
<br>
Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, Marketing, Computer Science or related field OR equivalent of 4 years of relevant experience required. 
<br>
<br>
MBA or Graduate degree preferred.  
Experience Minimum 5 years of experience in business development, sales, marketing or operations in the technology industry required. 
<br>
<br>
3-5 years of experience in the fields of high performance cloud computing, data center operations, data-intensive science, and/or genomics preferred. 
10+ years of experience in business development in the technology industry preferred. 
Experience working across a software ecosystem, channels and technology companies preferred.
