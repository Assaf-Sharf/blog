#### Requisition 095140

Responsible for managing information security during development, transition and production operations. Develop and manage a framework to deliver operational and technical solutions to mitigate threats while maximizing process automation and efficiency. Work cross-functionally to ensure alignment with other programs to protect the organization from operational, reputational, and financial loss. 
<br>
<br>
Ensure information security architecture and application decisions are implemented consistently. Recommend information security controls; including standards for information system architecture, operations, access, and use. Manage the information security impact of projects and programs, recommend approaches and approve security designs for new systems or infrastructure. Where no appropriate standard exists, develop a suitable standard and secure approval through appropriate governance channels. 
Foster increased security knowledge throughout all levels of the organization. Accept and successfully execute change while supporting employees through the process, and keeping them focused on priorities. Collaborate with required third parties on related security activities. 
Assists with identifying, reporting, and resolving security violations. 
<br>
<br>
Develop and manage the organization's information security policies. Develop metrics and reporting on security incidents, trends and exposures and rationalize true threat incidents with immediate action. Seek out industry trends and organizational knowledge to understand alternative approaches and solutions. 
<br>
<br>
Lead security reviews and risk assessments for critical systems and infrastructure. Continually assess vulnerability of security systems. Support a disaster recovery and business continuity plan. Ensure initiatives meet internal and external compliance requirements and maintain the information security status in line with stringent business risk standards. 
<br>
<br>
Bachelor of Science in a related field such as Computer Science, Information Science and Security OR four years of significant related professional experience required. 
<br>
Certification of CISSO, CISM, CHP, CGEIT, CSCS, CISSP, ISSAP preferred.  
<br>
5+ years of progressively responsible and directly related work experience. 
<br>
<br>
Experience and knowledge of security polices, procedures and controls; security standards, risk management, configuration management, security architectures, continuity of operations, contingency planning, disaster recovery, application security, and cyber-security rules, laws, and regulations required. 
<br>
Knowledge of HIPAA Privacy and Security regulations preferred.
