#### Requisition 095282

Designs, configures, manages and supports large-scale cloud computing infrastructure. We currently operate 7000+ cores and 5+ PB of cloud computing infrastructure, on mostly commodity hardware, with plans to at least double over the next year. 
<br>
<br>
The focus of this role is on hardware operations. This includes monitoring and troubleshooting hardware issues, tracking and processing RMAs, benchmarking, and keeping up to date with new hardware developments to help make purchasing recommendations. Additionally, this position will include taking responsibility for system monitoring, such as deploying monitoring software and providing reports on system status. 
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate is detail-oriented and enjoys working on all aspects of computer systems from low-level hardware to automating virtual machine deployments. The successful candidate will have a keen sense of how to automate tasks and work efficiently. This position requires excellent communication skills and working with a multi-disciplinary team.  
<br>
<br>
Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field OR three years of Linux systems administration experience required.
<br>
Minimum of two years of Linux systems administration experience required. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with Linux scripting and/or programming required. 
<br>
<br>
Experience replacing and installing rack-mounted hardware and components required. 
<br>
<br>
Familiarity with virtualization required. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with data center operations preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with monitoring technologies, such as Nagios, Fluentd or Logstash preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with automation technologies, such as Chef or Puppet preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with cloud software stacks such as OpenStack, Amazon AWS or Hadoop preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with distributed file systems, such as GlusterFS, Lustre or Ceph preferred.
