#### Requisition 095739

Responsible for managing all aspects of the Genomic Data Commons project to ensure the it meets contractual obligations and deliverables are met. Works closely with the technical leads to develop project plans and ensure each team is on track. Coordinates activities between the project team and other University groups including, but not limited to: IT Services, Purchasing, Finance, University Research Administration, Legal, and Administration. Gathers business requirements and ensures the University's IT Services networking and data center teams are able to appropriately respond . Works closely with the Director of Research, the lead of the infrastructure team, and the data import team to ensure close coordination and collaboration. Ensures the Director and other stakeholders are aware of all challenge and issues. Includes national and international projects.
<br>
<br>
Bachelor's degree in the field of IT/ Business Administration/ Project Management or equivalent of four years of relevant experience required.
<br>
<br>
Minimum of three years experience in a project management role required. 
<br>
<br>
Demonstrated experience coming into a project or team with minimal project management structure or documentation, evaluating the requirements and successfully gaining buying to implement a project management structure required. 
<br>
<br>
Knowledge of HIPAA and FISMA regulations preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience managing a project funded by a federal contract preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience working on research infrastructure preferred. 
<br>
<br>
Experience with both hardware and software IT projects preferred.
