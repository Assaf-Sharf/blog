title: Google will allow users to donate to charities directly through the Play Store
date: December 13, 2018 at 04:41AM
categories: Tech News
---
<img class="align-center" src="%20http://d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android-market_119259.png?width=36&amp;height=36&amp;mode=crop&amp;upscale=false">
<p><p>In time for the holidays, Google will launch the ability for Android users in select countries to donate to charity organizations directly through its Google Play app store for Android devices.</p>
<p>The feature was announced by Maxim Mai, <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/android-market/'><img alt='Small Google Play Store icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android-market_119259.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Google Play Store</a>'s Business Development Manager, in <a href="https://blog.google/products/google-play/give-back-charitable-donations-google-play/" rel="nofollow">an official Google blog post</a>. For users in Canada, France, Germany, Great Britain, Indonesia, Italy, Mexico, Spain, Taiwan, and the United States, the ability to donate to the following organizations will be coming &quot;over the next few days&quot;:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.redcross.org/" rel="nofollow">American Red Cross</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=3277" rel="nofollow">rated 84.09/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.charitywater.org/" rel="nofollow">charity: water</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=12548" rel="nofollow">rated 91.16/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/" rel="nofollow">Doctors Without Borders USA</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=3628" rel="nofollow">rated 94.74/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://girlswhocode.com/" rel="nofollow">Girls Who Code</a> (too recent a charity to be rated on Charity Navigator, though <a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.profile&amp;ein=300728021" rel="nofollow">its mission statement and key tenants are listed</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.rescue.org/" rel="nofollow">International Rescue Committee</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=3898" rel="nofollow">rated 91.16/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.roomtoread.org/" rel="nofollow">Room to Read</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=10217" rel="nofollow">rated 91.42/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.savethechildren.org/" rel="nofollow">Save the Children</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=4438" rel="nofollow">rated 88.30/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.unicef.org/" rel="nofollow">United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund</a> (UNICEF) (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=4617" rel="nofollow">rated 83.94/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.wfpusa.org/" rel="nofollow">World Food Program USA</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=7696" rel="nofollow">rated 85.41/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://www.worldwildlife.org/" rel="nofollow">World Wildlife Fund US</a> (<a href="https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&amp;orgid=4770" rel="nofollow">rated 82.32/100 on Charity Navigator</a>)</li>
</ul>
<p>Google states that 100% of donations will be given to the requested charity, with users only needing to visit <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/editorial_collection/promotion_topic_givingweek2018" rel="nofollow"><a href='http://play.google.com/donate' rel='nofollow'>http://play.google.com/donate </a></a> on an <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/android/'><img alt='Small Android icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android_84815.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Android</a> device of their choice in order to donate via the Play Store.</p>
