<p><p>Parallels, one of the most notorious pieces of software for desktop virtualization, is now under new ownership.</p>
<p>Corel, the developer of software such as <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/corel-draw/'><img alt='Small CorelDRAW Graphics Suite icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/corel-draw_90601.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />CorelDRAW Graphics Suite</a> and <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/ulead-videostudio/'><img alt='Small Corel VideoStudio icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/ulead-videostudio_45718.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Corel VideoStudio</a>, has announced that it's acquiring the Parallels IP and its developers. The software that Corel now owns includes the following:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/parallels-desktop/'><img alt='Small Parallels Desktop icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/parallels-desktop_120395.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Parallels Desktop</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/parallels-toolbox/'><img alt='Small Parallels Toolbox icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/parallels-toolbox_120842.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Parallels Toolbox</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/parallels-access/'><img alt='Small Parallels Access icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/parallels-access_32000.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Parallels Access</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/parallels-business-automation--standard/'>Parallels Business Automation</a></li>
</ul>
<p>These, alongside every other piece of Parallels software, were acquired by Corel for an undisclosed amount of money. The only financial details disclosed were that Corel &quot;plans significant investment&quot; into the Parallels, with plans to release products on <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/android/'><img alt='Small Android icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android_84815.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Android</a>, Chromebooks, <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/ios/'><img alt='Small iOS icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/ios_88776.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />iOS</a>, <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/linux-kernel/'><img alt='Small Linux kernel icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/linux-kernel_97656.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Linux kernel</a>, <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/mac-os/'><img alt='Small macOS icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/mac-os_132940.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />macOS</a>, Windows, and the cloud.</p>
