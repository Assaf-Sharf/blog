---
title: "GHRS: Graph-based Hybrid Recommendation System with Application to Movie Recommendation"
---

ABSTRACT
Research about recommender systems emerges over the last decade and comprises valuable services to increase different companies' revenue. While most existing recommender systems rely either on a content-based approach or a collaborative approach, there are hybrid approaches that can improve recoomendation system method using a graph-based model associated with the similarity of user's rating in combination with users' demographic and location information.
