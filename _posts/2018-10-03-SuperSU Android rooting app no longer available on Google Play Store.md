<img class="align-center" src="%20http://d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android-market_119259.png?width=36&amp;height=36&amp;mode=crop&amp;upscale=false">
<p><p>SuperSU, for years one of the most popular rooting applications for Android, has been delisted from the Google Play Store today.</p>
<p>The developer of SuperSU, Chainfire, ceased developing it a year ago on October 3rd, 2017. Since then, its current owner, Coding Code Mobile Technology, has not updated it or posted news on any of its social media accounts. This, coupled with today's removal from the <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/android-market/'><img alt='Small Google Play Store icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android-market_119259.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Google Play Store</a>, signifies that users looking for superuser access and the ability to root their <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/android/'><img alt='Small Android icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/android_84815.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Android</a> devices should look for alternatives.</p>
<p>Though the free version of <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/supersu/'><img alt='Small SuperSU icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/supersu_98226.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />SuperSU</a> has been delisted, as of this news post, the $3.75 <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.chainfire.supersu.pro" rel="nofollow">SuperSU Pro</a> is still available for purchase and download. For viable free alternatives, apps such as the open-source <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/magisk/'><img alt='Small Magisk icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/magisk_110090.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Magisk</a> are still actively updated and available.</p>
