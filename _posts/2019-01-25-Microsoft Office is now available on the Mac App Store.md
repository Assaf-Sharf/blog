<p><p>After a long period of availability on macOS, Microsoft's Office suite is finally available for download from Apple's Mac App Store.</p>
<p>Previously, <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-office-suite/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office Suite icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-office-suite_68400.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office Suite</a> was only available on <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/mac-os/'><img alt='Small macOS icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/mac-os_132940.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />macOS</a> via downloading directly from Microsoft's website. This shift lowers the barrier to entry for more casual computer users that only download and install new applications from the <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/mac-app-store/'><img alt='Small Mac App Store icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/mac-app-store_99939.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Mac App Store</a> and not from other online sources.</p>
<p>Pricing for Microsoft Office remains the same as before its App Store debut, with the personal edition costing $70 USD a year being good for a single user, with the $100 USD a year home edition covering up to six users. <a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-office-365/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office 365 icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-office-365_72546.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office 365</a> on the App Store includes the following apps:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-word/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office Word icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-word_118084.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office Word</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-excel/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office Excel icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-excel_30420.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office Excel</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-powerpoint/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office Powerpoint icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-powerpoint_118085.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office Powerpoint</a></li>
<li><a href='//alternativeto.net/software/microsoft-office-outlook/'><img alt='Small Microsoft Office Outlook icon' class='mini-app-icon' src='//d2.alternativeto.net/dist/icons/microsoft-office-outlook_118086.png?width=36&height=36&mode=crop&upscale=false' />Microsoft Office Outlook</a></li>
