#### Requisition 097475

Design, configure and monitor security system infrastructure and hardening systems against threats. 

Create and manage an enterprise process for identifying, prioritizing, and remediating system security gaps and weaknesses.  Work with existing security policies to maintain standards and procedures.  Maintain security controls to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the system.   Mitigate risks through incident handling/incident response/incident investigation.  Test security products and systems to detect security weakness.  Respond to security audits, assessment and conducts risk assessments.  Support security planning, risk analysis and risk management.  Maintain accurate and current documentation for mandated regulatory standard(s).  Perform related duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
<br>
Bachelor of Science in a related field such as Computer Science, Information Science and Security OR four (4) years of substantial related professional experience required. 
<br>
<br>
Certification of one (1) or more of: CompTIA Security+, Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), GIAC Security Essentials (GSEC) preferred.  
<br>
<br>
Minimum of three (3) years of progressively responsible and directly related work experience required. 
<br>
<br>
Experience working with the following security and compliance frameworks: NIST, FISMA, HIPAA preferred. 
Knowledge in the implementation and use of information security processes and tools (e.g. Encryption solutions, change detection and SEIM tools, vulnerability scanning, mobile and cloud integration, forensics, incident response) preferred. 
Knowledge of network security systems such as firewalls, VPN's, PKI, and intrusion detection (Cisco ASA, Palo Alto Networks) preferred. 
Experience with Enterprise Governance Risk & Compliance (eGRC) systems preferred. 
Experience with current security designs, technologies and products that support security requirements preferred. 
Programming experience in languages such as Bourne Shell, PERL, or .NET preferred.
