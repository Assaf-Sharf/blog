What is Misinformation?

It is evident that, with the sheer amount of discourse taking place on social media platforms, seemingly unbelievable misinformation can slip through the cracks of moderation and explode in popularity. Claims disputing the true lethality of the COVID-19 coronavirus or the validity of the 2020 election have taken sites like Twitter and Reddit by storm, prompting data science communities to analyze how such claims were conceived and spread. We tried to explore how these kinds of claims spread, if at all, and compared these three different form of diffusion to each other.
